Answer Key

Q1: Good conductors of heat
Metals are known for their ability to conduct heat and electricity efficiently.

Q2: Oxygen
Oxygen is a non-metal, while iron, copper, and aluminum are all metals.

Q3: Gas
Many non-metals, such as oxygen and nitrogen, are gases at room temperature.

Q4: Mercury
Mercury is the only metal that is liquid at room temperature.

Q5: Brittle
Non-metals are usually brittle in solid form and cannot be easily shaped like metals.
